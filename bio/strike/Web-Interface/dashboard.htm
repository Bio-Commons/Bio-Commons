Stage 1: Soil Sample Dilution X

It is important to dilute the soil so that your petri dish does not get overcrowded with too many cultures which makes it difficult to identify them.

  1. Prepare sterilized test tubes containing 9ml water each
  2. Count and label them (eg. 6 test tubes, T1 to T6)

    You added these test tubes:

    • T1 [remove]
    • T2 [remove]
    • T3 [remove]
    • T4 [remove]
    • T5 [remove]

  3. Place 1g of soil sample into the first test tube (T1)
  4. Swirl test tube to mix solution well
  5. Take 1ml out of the test tube and place diluted sample into the next one and swirl to mix solution well
  6. (1ml from T1 to T2, 1ml from T2, to T3 and so on)
  7. Repeat above step until all test tubes contain a sample
